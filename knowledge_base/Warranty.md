# TechMart Electronics Warranty Policy

## 1-Year Limited Warranty
All electronic hardware products sold under the TechMart brand or purchased through authorized TechMart retail outlets carry a 1-Year Limited Manufacturer's Warranty starting from the invoice date.

## What is Covered
This warranty covers:
1. Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship.
2. Hardware failures under normal, recommended operating conditions.
3. Defective components (e.g., failure of screen backlights, charging ports, internal circuit boards).

## What is NOT Covered
This warranty does not cover:
1. Accidental damage (e.g., cracked screens, drops, liquid spills).
2. Damage caused by natural disasters (fire, lightning, floods).
3. Software corruption, virus infections, or unauthorized operating system modifications (e.g., rooting or jailbreaking).
4. Repairs or modifications carried out by unauthorized third-party technicians.
5. Normal wear and tear (e.g., battery degradation, scratches).

## How to Claim Warranty
To file a warranty claim:
1. Locate your original order ID or purchase invoice.
2. Visit `techmartelectronics.com/warranty` and fill out the claim form, describing the defect.
3. If troubleshooting fails, we will issue a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
4. Ship the item back to our repair center. We cover return shipping if the claim is filed within 90 days of purchase.
5. We will repair, replace with an identical/refurbished unit, or issue a refund at our discretion within 10 business days of receiving the device.
