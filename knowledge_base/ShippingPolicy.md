# TechMart Electronics Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options & Rates
We offer several shipping methods within the United States:
- **Standard Shipping (3-5 business days)**: Free for orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat fee of $4.99 is charged.
- **Express Shipping (2 business days)**: Flat fee of $12.99.
- **Overnight Shipping (1 business day)**: Flat fee of $24.99. Free for TechMart Premium Members.

## Order Processing Time
- All orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on business days (Monday through Friday) are processed and shipped the same day.
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST, or during weekends and federal holidays, will be processed and shipped the following business day.

## Shipping Restrictions
- We ship to PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses using Standard Shipping only. Express and Overnight shipping are not available for these addresses.
- International shipping is available to Canada, the UK, Australia, and the EU. International shipping fees vary by location and are calculated at checkout. Custom duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

## Order Tracking
Once your package is handed over to the courier (UPS, FedEx, or USPS), you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to trace the delivery.
