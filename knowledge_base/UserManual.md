# TechMart Smart Hub (Model SH-200) User Manual

## Product Overview
The TechMart Smart Hub (SH-200) is the central controller for your smart home ecosystem. It connects smart plugs, lights, cameras, and sensors into a single interface.

## Quick Start Guide
1. Connect the power adapter to the SH-200 micro-USB port and plug it into a wall outlet.
2. The LED ring will glow solid yellow during boot. Wait 60 seconds until the LED starts blinking blue, indicating it is in pairing mode.
3. Open the TechMart Smart Home App on your mobile device and tap **Add Device (+)**.
4. Select **Smart Hub SH-200** and follow the on-screen instructions to connect it to your 2.4GHz Wi-Fi network (5GHz is not supported).

## Troubleshooting & Reset Guide

### LED Indicator Codes
- **Blinking Blue**: Ready for Wi-Fi pairing.
- **Solid Blue**: Connected to Wi-Fi and online.
- **Blinking Red**: Wi-Fi connection lost. Check router.
- **Solid Red**: Hardware error or boot failure.

### Wi-Fi Connection Failures
If the Smart Hub fails to connect to Wi-Fi:
1. Ensure your smartphone is connected to a 2.4GHz Wi-Fi network during setup.
2. Move the Smart Hub closer to your router (within 10 feet during setup).
3. Restart your internet router and try again.

### Factory Reset Instructions
If your Smart Hub becomes unresponsive or you want to connect it to a new Wi-Fi network:
1. Locate the physical **Reset Button** on the bottom of the device (a tiny pinhole).
2. Insert a paperclip or SIM ejector tool into the hole.
3. Press and hold the reset button for **10 full seconds**.
4. The LED ring will blink red three times and then turn solid yellow.
5. Release the button. The Smart Hub will reboot and enter Wi-Fi pairing mode (blinking blue) within 2 minutes. All custom settings will be cleared.
