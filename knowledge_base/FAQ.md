## Product Pricing & Inventory
### Do you match competitor prices?
Yes, we offer a Price Match Guarantee. If you find a lower price on an identical in-stock item from an authorized competitor within 14 days of your purchase, show us the ad, and we will match it. This does not apply to clearance sales, open-box items, or auction sites.

### What is the Premium Membership?
Our TechMart Premium Membership costs $9.99 per month or $99 annually. It includes:
- Free 1-day shipping on all eligible products.
- Exclusive member-only discounts.
- Extended 60-day return policy (regular policy is 30 days).
- Dedicated 24/7 priority customer support line.
