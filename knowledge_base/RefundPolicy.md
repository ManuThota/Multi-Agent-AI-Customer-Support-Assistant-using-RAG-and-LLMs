# TechMart Electronics Refund and Return Policy

## 30-Day Return Window
Customers can return any product purchased from TechMart within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. For TechMart Premium members, this return window is extended to 60 days.

## Conditions for Return
To be eligible for a full refund, items must meet the following criteria:
1. The product must be in its original packaging with all original accessories, manuals, and warranty registration cards.
2. The product must have no signs of physical abuse, water damage, or unauthorized disassembly.
3. A valid proof of purchase (receipt, invoice, or digital order ID) must be provided.

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned or refunded:
- Digital download software or activation keys.
- Gift cards.
- Consumable goods (e.g., thermal paste, printer ink, open batteries) unless they are defective out-of-box.

## Restocking Fees
- A 10% restocking fee is applied to open-box laptops, desktop PCs, smart televisions, and high-end cameras unless the return is due to a hardware defect.
- Standard items do not incur any restocking fees.

## Refund Process
Once we receive your return package at our warehouse, inspection takes 3 to 5 business days. Once approved, refunds are credited back to the original payment method:
- Credit Card/Debit Card: 5 to 7 business days.
- PayPal: 1 to 2 business days.
- Store Credit: Instant.
